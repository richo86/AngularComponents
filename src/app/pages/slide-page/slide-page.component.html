Slide Component Demo

Add a custom slide switch to your Angular app. This demo shows how to use and customize the slide component.

Usage Instructions

To use the slide component, import the SlideComponent and add it to your template: 


    <app-slide [(checked)]="isToggled">Slide me!</app-slide>
    <p>Current state: {{ isToggled ? 'Enabled' : 'Disabled' }}</p>

The checked property supports two-way binding for reactive updates.

Component Details

  • HTML Structure: Custom slide switch and label.
  • CSS Styling: Modern switch styles, focus, and selection effects.
  • TypeScript Logic: Reactive toggle and event emission.

Easily customize the styles and behaviors to fit your needs.
