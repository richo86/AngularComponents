Usage Instructions

To use the expansion panel, import the ExpansionPanelComponent and add it to your template: 


    <app-expansion-panel>
      <div panelTitle>
        <h3>Click to Expand</h3>
      </div>
      <div panelContent>
        <p>Expanded content here.</p>
      </div>
    </app-expansion-panel>

The panel displays a summary and reveals more details when expanded.

Component Details

  • HTML Structure: Header and content projection for flexible layouts.
  • CSS Styling: Smooth transitions, custom themes, and responsive design.
  • TypeScript Logic: Expand/collapse state management and animation.

Customize colors, icons, and transitions in expansion-panel.component.css.
